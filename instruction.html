Instructions

Game Rules:

  • Get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  • Dealer must hit until they have at least 17.
  • Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card) pays 3:2.

How to Play:

Hit and Stand:

  • Hit: Take another card to try to get closer to 21.
  • Stand: Keep your current hand and end your turn.

Double:

  • Double your initial bet and receive exactly one more card.
  • Only available on your first two cards.

Split:

  • If you have two cards of the same value, you can split them, remove one card and choose a different card instead.

Surrender:

  • Forfeit half your bet and end your hand immediately.
  • Only available on your first two cards.

Leaderboard:

  • Top 25 players with the highest winnings will be displayed on the leaderboard.
  • Winnings are calculated based on the total amount won.
